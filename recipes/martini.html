Perfect Martini

Description

A classic cocktail, perfectly balanced with a high ABV.
Also known as the King of Cocktails.

  • Dry Gin
  • Dry Vermouth
  • Optional: Olives
  1. Add ice to mixing glass
  2. Pour 20ml of Gin and Vermouth into the mixing glass and stir gently
  3. Strain into Martini Glass
  4. Throw in an olive if you'd like and enjoy!
