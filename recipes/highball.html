Whisky Highball

Description

A refreshing combination of whisky and soda.
Perfect for any occasion.

  • Bourbon Whisky
  • Club Soda
  • Ice Cubes
  1. Add ice to tall glass
  2. Pour 30ml of whisky into the glass
  3. Pour in soda and stir gently
  4. Throw in a slice of lemon if you'd like and enjoy!
